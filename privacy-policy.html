Overview

Dark Mode Everywhere is a Chrome extension that applies dark mode to websites. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

Dark Mode Everywhere does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension does not track your browsing history, collect analytics, or send any information to external servers.

Local Storage

The extension stores your dark mode preferences (which websites you have enabled or disabled dark mode for) locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your dark mode preferences locally on your device.
  • ActiveTab: To detect the current website and apply dark mode.
  • Scripting: To inject the dark mode CSS into web pages.
  • Host permissions: To apply dark mode automatically on all websites.

Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties. There are no analytics, advertising, or tracking services used by this extension.

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
